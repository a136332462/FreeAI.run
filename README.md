## Overview

**FreeAI.run** is a curated AI directory built around a simple promise: help people discover the best free AI tools and websites across all categories.

The tagline is not only a slogan. It also explains the product direction:

- "Discover The Best" means the site is trying to filter, structure, and surface better options instead of dumping everything into a generic list.
- "Free AI Tools & Websites" makes the core focus clear: users come here first to discover useful AI products that are accessible, not only premium enterprise software.
- "Across all categories" signals breadth. The site is meant to cover productivity, creativity, content creation, development, and other major AI use cases in one place.

People usually describe it in three ways:

- As a user-facing directory, it is a clean place to browse AI tools for productivity, creativity, and content creation.
- As a growth channel for founders, it is a place to submit products, buy visibility, and keep listings discoverable over time.
- As a content-driven website, it combines category pages, product pages, curated collections, and multilingual routing in a way that feels structured and easy to explore.

It is especially useful for AI founders, indie makers, SaaS teams, marketers, and anyone trying to compare AI products without digging through low-quality list pages.

## What the Main Pages Do

- The homepage is the main discovery layer for search, trending tools, and featured visibility.

- The best AI tools pages work as curated recommendation pages with stronger editorial intent.

- Product detail pages give each tool its own searchable landing page.
- Search and Explore support users with strong intent who already know what they want.
- Skills pages add long-form topical depth beyond directory listings.
- Submit and Advertise pages convert product owners into free submitters, paid listers, or advertisers.

## How Different People Describe FreeAI.run

### As a user

FreeAI.run feels like a practical AI discovery site. Instead of mixing everything into one endless list, it separates free tools, curated recommendations, monthly new tools, open-source projects, discounts, and category pages so people can get to the right tools faster.

### As a product founder

FreeAI.run works like a visibility platform. A founder can submit a product for free, upgrade to stronger listing plans, or run short-term ad campaigns across homepage placements, listing feeds, and product detail pages.

### As a marketer

FreeAI.run is a focused traffic source. It gives marketers multiple ways to position a product in front of users who are already browsing AI tools, whether the goal is awareness, recurring exposure, or campaign-style launches.

### As a publisher or content operator

FreeAI.run looks like a structured content property rather than a thin landing page. It has homepage discovery, category hubs, product detail pages, monthly archives, curated collections, and a Skills content layer, which makes the site easier to navigate and easier to understand at a glance.
